## QA/Testing Lead

### Role Summary
Ensures thorough test coverage, manages QA activities, and validates that delivered features meet acceptance criteria.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate manual and automated testing
- Ensure quality standards and test coverage
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers to clarify acceptance

### Goals
- Prevent regressions and critical bugs in production
- Foster a culture of quality

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and result reports
- Feature acceptance sign-off

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
Designs user experiences, flows, and interfaces; advocates for usability throughout development.

### Responsibilities
- Produce design specs and user flows
- Collaborate on requirements with Product Managers and Developers
- Solicit feedback through prototypes or user testing

### Goals
- Improve product usability and adoption
- Reduce friction for end users

### Typical Communication
- Design review meetings
- Annotated wireframes/mockups

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Bridges the gap between business needs and technical implementation by defining requirements and clarifying scope.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document requirements
- Facilitate requirements review sessions
- Map user stories and use cases

### Goals
- Increase solution fit for business/customer needs

### Typical Communication
- Requirements docs
- User story workshops

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Coordinates deployment and release activities to ensure reliable, predictable deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Manage release plans and schedules
- Confirm pre-release checklists are complete
- Communicate status during releases

### Goals
- Minimize release risk
- Streamline and standardize deployment

### Typical Communication
- Release status updates
- Coordination with Dev/QA/Stakeholders

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Maintains accurate user and project documentation and assists with onboarding enablement.

### Responsibilities
- Write and update documentation
- Collaborate with teams for technical accuracy

### Goals
- Reduce onboarding time
- Improve self-serviceability of knowledge

### Typical Communication
- Documentation pull requests
- Async updates and guides

---

## Key Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Provides business or customer perspective and has authority for major approvals/escalations.

### Responsibilities
- Attend major milestone reviews
- Approve deliverables or resolve priority conflicts

### Goals
- Ensure the solution meets organizational goals

### Typical Communication
- Major project update briefings
- Decision logs
